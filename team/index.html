Richard‌
Wengle

Established in March 1992, the firm of Richard Wengle Architect Inc. is strongly committed to excellence in architectural design and technology, and highly values client satisfaction from concept design to occupancy and use. The trademarks of the practice are the creativity, practicality and efficiency with which each project is developed and detailed. In collaboration with the construction team, this results in iconic, elegant and timeless architecture.

Brian‌
Gluckstein

Acclaimed designer Brian Gluckstein is renowned for creating luxurious, livable interiors. As principal of Gluckstein Design, his portfolio includes residential projects in cities such as Aspen, Palm Beach, Miami and New York, and high-profile commercial projects such as the Four Seasons Hotel Palm Beach. Brian is internationally recognized as one of the top thirty-five designers in the world by Andrew Martin’s Interior Design Journal. Brian lives and works in Toronto, New York, and Palm Beach.

Michael‌
London

Michael London Design’s portfolio of high-profile multi-unit residences, commercial interiors inspired with iconic luxury brands, restaurants, and private residences are situated within some of the most prestigious communities in the greater Toronto area. The motivation behind Michael London Design is to achieve a high standard of design that is immersive, creative, and inspired by culture which is fundamental in the pursuit of achieving unique interiors.

Janet‌
Rosenberg‌
& Studio

Janet Rosenberg & Studio Inc. is one of Canada’s most distinguished landscape architecture and urban design studios recognized for its extensive and award-winning portfolio of work that includes public realm spaces as well as private residential gardens, green roofs, and condominium towers. The Studio creates treasured, ecologically-responsible landscapes that respond to the demands of the urban environment, and engage, excite and enhance the quality of life for those who inhabit them.

Baker Real‌
Estate

For over 25 years, Baker Real Estate has specialized in the planning, sales, and marketing of luxury residential and resort developments, with offices in Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver, and internationally in Shanghai. Led by Barbara Lawlor, President and CEO, and a prestigious team of professionals, along with their proprietary and innovative technologies and unparalleled experience, success in every new challenge and record-breaking sellouts affords Baker Real Estate the title of industry leader.

The Forest‌
Hill Group

The Forest Hill Group is the leading provider of luxury concierge, bespoke security, upscale valet parking, premium maintenance and complete property services to condominium communities in Toronto. Offering only the utmost attention to detail and professionalism, The Forest Hill Group is an industry leader in first-class services to clients at luxury rental properties, commercial buildings, upscale retail locations, and Canada’s largest sports and entertainment venues since 2001.
